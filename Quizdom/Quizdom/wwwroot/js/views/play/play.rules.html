How to play
  1. Active player picks a question
  2. Question and 4 answers are shown
  3. Every player selects an answer
  4. All players who answer correctly are awarded points
  5. Player with quickest correct answer picks next question
    If nobody answers correctly, active player picks again
Winning
  • For every question, fastest correct player awarded bonus points
  • Players ranked by points, questions correct, and response time
